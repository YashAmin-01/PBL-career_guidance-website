Bachelors in Business Administration

BBA Marketing

This degree is designed to give a broad knowledge of the functional aspects of a company and their interconnection, while also allowing specialization in a particular area.

About this Course

Marketing careers include personal sales in retail and commercial environments, new product planning for major marketers, consulting organisations, and advertising agencies in manufacturing firms and service providers, and marketing research. Marketing specialisation courses concentrate on particular marketing subjects, such as marketing analysis, advertisement, personal sales and delivery across networks. Students who have successfully completed the programme for this major would have gained expertise in the application of marketing theory and have achieved maturity in analytical reasoning, quantitative research, and oral and written communication objectives for general education.

Subjects

Some of the major topics which will be covered in the training for BBA in marketing are Market analysis, Product management, Creating marketing strategies, Consumer behaviour, Promotion, Market research, Branding, Collecting customer feedback. Besides this, the program also includes many practical classes as well.

Jobs and Recruitments

Those who complete this course can work with banks like SBI and the Deutsche Bank and also with multi-national companies like Nestle, Mudra, Deloitte, Amazon, Capgemini, TCS, Wipro Limited, etc. They get to work on profiles like Marketing Executive, Advertising Executive, Sales Manager, Market Research Analyst, etc.
The average starting salary is INR 3-6 Lakhs.
